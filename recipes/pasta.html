Pasta

Description

Pasta is a staple of Italian cuisine, made from durum wheat flour and water, and typically formed into various shapes. It is often served with a variety of sauces, such as marinara, alfredo, or pesto. Pasta is one of the most versatile foods, enjoyed worldwide in many forms like spaghetti, fettuccine, ravioli, and lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: Made from semolina flour, eggs, and water (for traditional types like spaghetti, fettuccine, or ravioli).
  • Sauces: Common sauces include tomato-based (marinara), cream-based (alfredo), and pesto (made with basil, garlic, and pine nuts).
  • Cheese: Parmesan, ricotta, or mozzarella are often added either in the sauce or as a topping.
  • Herbs and spices: Garlic, basil, oregano, and thyme are common additions to pasta dishes.

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare your sauce (such as saut√©ing garlic for a tomato sauce or creating a creamy alfredo sauce).
  3. Drain the pasta and mix it with the sauce.
  4. Top with grated cheese, herbs, and any additional ingredients (like vegetables or meat).
  5. Serve and enjoy!
