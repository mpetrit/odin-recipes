Pizza

Description

Pizza is a savory dish from Italy that consists of a usually round base of dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and a variety of other ingredients. It is typically baked in an oven, though variations such as deep-dish pizza exist. It has become one of the most popular foods globally, often customized with numerous toppings to cater to different tastes.

Ingredients

  • Dough: The base of the pizza, typically made of flour, water, yeast, and salt.
  • Tomato Sauce: A key ingredient for the base flavor.
  • Cheese: Most commonly mozzarella, but variations exist like cheddar or goat cheese.
  • Toppings: Include a variety of meats (pepperoni, sausage), vegetables (mushrooms, olives), and others like pineapple, anchovies, etc.

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough and spread it out on a pizza pan or stone.
  2. Top the dough with tomato sauce, cheese, and other toppings.
  3. Bake the pizza in a hot oven (typically 475-500°F/250°C).
  4. Remove from the oven, let cool slightly, and slice to serve.
